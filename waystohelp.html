What can you do?

We as a collective have a role in society to help the penguins.

Use less plastic

As many of you know, only about 9% of the plastic we use is actually recyled. So if you plan to use plastic, please recycle. It all lands in landfills, which end up in the oceans.

Over Fishing is a big problem

Over fishing depletes the krill and other small fish populations, which penguins eat and need to survive. If you know of any companies fishing in an area that is close to penguins, please remind them of this issue!

Educate yourself

The more you understand about penguins, the more you can help!

Oil Spills

If an oil spill has happened near you, help out with clean up! Talk to others and educate others on the issue and how to help!

Donate to our website

100% of our donations go towards helping the penguins!
